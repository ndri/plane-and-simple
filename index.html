Plane and Simple

A simple 3D plane game made as a computer graphics project with Three.js and Cannon.js.

Made by Andri Soone, Jorgen Juurik and Erik Martin Vetemaa.

Controls: w/s - throttle, q/e - yaw, left/right - roll, up/down - pitch, space - reset position
